About Myself

Hello Everyone, I am glad to introduce myself and tell you more about my education, work experience I have. I developed this website for demonstration and informative purposes so that everyone could get to know me better and take a look at the projects I have done so far. As for me, I am originally from Russia – from Eastern part of Russia, from southern Siberia. I am from big city Irkutsk (600,000 people) located very close to the fresh water lake Baikal, which are not far from Mongolia and China. I have always been interested in computer technologies and when I was in Russia I got my first Bachelor’s Degree in Information Systems and Economics. Beside just education I have also worked on improving my professional skills on practice. My first job in Russia was related with network support and configuration. I always wanted to move forward and get more achievements in my life. Right after graduation from Russia College I improved the knowledge of my English language, passed all necessary exams in order to continue my education and work experience in the United States. So I came to Fargo, North Dakota in July of 2007 and started education at Minnesota State University Moorhead at that time. Since I had already had Bachelor’s degree from Russian College, I transferred my main courses from my prior degree to MSUM and focused only on computer science classes. As a result of this, I was able to get my second Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Information Systems from MSUM in two years only. While I was still in college I also worked for North Dakota Trade Office as a Computer Specialist. I assisted the company with various projects and implemented additional functionality on their website. After I graduated from MSUM I was hired by Titan Machinery as a help desk and network support specialist. I answered phone calls and resolved different technical questions over the phone and in person. After working for Titan Machinery for one year I was then promoted to another position to work as a Program Analyst on their new Enterprise Resource Planning system – JD Edwards Oracle E1. I was in charge of getting requirements from company employees, writing technical documentation and developing different applications, reports for JD Edwards ERP system. I worked for Titan Machinery almost 6 years as a Program Analyst and after their ERP system was successfully implemented I decided to move forward and get more advanced degree at North Dakota State University. Currently, I pursue Master of Software Engineering degree at NDSU and I also work part-time as an Account Specialist at Swanson Health Products. I switched to work for Swanson because they provide for me flexible hours and provide conditions so that I could go to school and work for convenient for me schedule. I assist Swanson International department with various tasks such as processing orders from international customers, preparing international documentation and providing technical support for their software. Beside school and work I also take active part in different computer related events in Fargo such as local hackathons. In my spare time, I work on different projects including my start-up web applications: nutrilookup.com and censusApp. I proposed these two applications on hackathon events and now I just improving them for end users. For more information about my education and work experience please see my resume and school transcripts below:

State Reserve of Russian Federation

State Reserves of Russian Federation is Government Company that stores and maintains various reserves in case of any unexpected hazard incidents such as floods, storms and economic downfalls – anything that can significantly damage the local region and population. In case of any hazard incidents, the Federal Reserve provides necessary support to the local community. I worked for one of such agencies, located in my home city Irkutsk. I was in charge of configuration and support of computer network, consisting of 40 computers and two servers. One server was dedicated for Window Domain, Active directory, Shared Printers and Security Policies. Another Server was set up as Company Mail Server to send and receive email within Rosreserve internal network. Beside network support I also provided training and consultation for company employees, maintained Russian ERP system “1C Enterprise”. For more information please see my resume.
