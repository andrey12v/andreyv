About Myself

Hello Everyone, I am glad to introduce myself and tell you more about my education, work experience I have. I developed this website for demonstration and informative purposes so that everyone could get to know me better and take a look at the projects I have done so far. This website was developed from the scratch without using Word Press or any other content management tools.
As for me, I am originally from Eastern part of Russia, from Siberia. I am from big city Irkutsk with population of about 600,000 people. The city is located very close to the biggest fresh water lake Baikal which are not far from Mongolia and China as you can see on this Google Map.
I have always been interested in computer technologies and when I was in Russia I got my first Bachelor’s Degree in Information Systems and Economics. Beside just education I have also worked on improving my professional skills on practice. My first job in Russia was related with network support and configuration. I always wanted to move forward and get more achievements in my life. Right after graduation from Russia College I improved the knowledge of my English language, passed all necessary exams in order to continue my education in the United States. So I came to Fargo, North Dakota in July of 2007 and started education at Minnesota State University Moorhead. Since I already had Bachelor’s degree from Russian College, I transferred my main courses from my prior degree to MSUM and focused only on computer science classes. As a result of this, I was able to get my second Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Information Systems from MSUM in two years only. While I was still in college I also worked for North Dakota Trade Office as a Computer Specialist. I assisted the company with various projects such as website development and computer support. After I graduated from MSUM I was hired by Titan Machinery as a help desk and network support specialist. I answered phone calls and resolved different technical questions over the phone and in person. After working for Titan Machinery for one year I was then promoted to another position to work as a Program Analyst on their new Enterprise Resource Planning system – JD Edwards Oracle E1. I was in charge of getting requirements from company employees, writing technical documentation and developing different applications, reports for JD Edwards ERP system. I worked for Titan Machinery almost 6 years as a Program Analyst and after their ERP system was successfully implemented I decided to move forward and get more advanced degree at North Dakota State University. Currently, I purse Master of Software Engineering degree at NDSU and I also work at Swanson Health Products as an International Account Specialist. Since Swanson provided form me flexible work hours this company became an excellent place for me to work while getting my master degree at NDSU. Working with customers on the international level and processing their bulk orders, I was also able to get very valuable and interesting work experience at Swanson that I have never had before.
Beside school and work, I also take active part in different computer related events in Fargo such as local hackathons and game development competitions. I have been involved in two hackathon events where I proposed the applications: nutrilookup and censusApp. Beside this, I was also involved in one game development event in Fargo where I implemented “Moby Dick” video game, developed based on C# programming language and Unity Studio Tools.
