About Myself

Hello everyone, I am glad to introduce myself and share more about my education and work experience I have. During my time as a graduate student at NDSU, I developed this website for demonstration and informative purposes so that everyone could get to know more about me, and take a look at some of my projects I have done at school and work.
I have always been passionate about computer technologies and continuously expanded my knowledge in various areas, from network configuration to software development. I earned my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Information Systems from Minnesota State University Moorhead in 2009. While in college, I worked part-time as a Web Developer. After graduation, I have worked full-time for various companies in Fargo, North Dakota as an IT Specialist, System Analyst and Software Developer. Seeking to enhance my qualifications, I later obtained a Master’s Degree in Software Engineering from North Dakota State University.
Beside school and work, I have also taken active part in different computer events in Fargo such as hackathons and game development competitions. At hackathons, I proposed such applications as nutrilookup and censusApp. Additionally, during a game development competition in Fargo, I created the “Moby Dick” video game using C# programming language and Unity Visual Studio.

Online Registration & Reports

North Dakota Trade Office (NDTO) is a membership-based, public company that helps North Dakota companies to grow their export business by providing them education, research, engagement, advocacy, and expertise.
NDTO is dedicated to expanding trade partnerships between North Dakota and the world and does so by facilitating business between North Dakota’s global companies and their clients through programs and services including Trade Missions, Reverse Trade Missions and the annual Big Iron Farm Show’s International Visitors Program. NDTO also provides educational tools for North Dakota companies and other international business professionals.
I worked for the North Dakota Trade Office part-time as a Web Developer while I was a student at MSUM. I implemented different functionality on the company website such as Big Iron Registration System, Dynamic Photo Gallery and English/Russian Translation Tool using C# and MS SQL Server technologies.

International Marketing Systems

"International Marketing Systems" company specializes in marketing and customer relations. The IMS sells products across multiple online venues both domestically and abroad. The company offers affordable personalized solutions that give customers’ online stores - the competitive edge they need. The IMS team constantly expands customers’ clientele and establishes a reliable image for companies and their products/services.
I worked for IMS as a Software Developer on a contract-basis and implemented for the company such projects as inventory scanning application using barcode reader and various reports in the Inventory Tracking System (used C# and MS SQL Server).

Titan Machinery

Titan Machinery is one of the largest dealerships of Case IH and New Holland equipment in the USA. At the time, when I graduated from college in 2009 Titan Machinery had already had about 40 stores and was one of the largest dealerships in the Midwest. The company expanded to more than 100 stores domestically and internationally afterwards.
Titan Machinery hired me to work for them as an IT Help Desk after I graduated from MSUM. I provided technical support, configured computer networks, fixed PCs, laptops and printers. Since the company expanded very quickly they also worked on the replacement of their legacy ERP system with a new, more advanced ERP which needed for their growing business. After one year of IT help desk work the company promoted me to work as a System Analyst on their new ERP system “Oracle JD Edwards Enterprise1”. As a System Analyst, I talked to stakeholders, elicited business requirements, wrote technical documentation and implemented JDE reports and applications.

Swanson Health Products

Swanson Health Products is one of the largest companies in Fargo that produces and sells healthy vitamins and supplements
I worked for Swanson part-time as an Account Representative at their International Department, when I went to North Dakota State University for a Master's Degree in Software Engineering. Working as an Account Representative I was in touch with customers, processed bulk orders in Swanson’s ERP system and prepared various export documentation such as shipping, commercial invoices, and certificates of analysis.

Sundog

Sundog Interactive is a marketing consulting company that provides a wide range of marketing services from advertisement, market research and promotions to web and salesforce CRM development.
I worked for Sundog as a Salesforce Software Engineer. I customized Salesforce (Classic, Lightning) CRM system for Sundog clients and earned a Salesforce Administrator Certificate, afterwards.

Bushel

Bushel is an independently owned software company and a leading provider of software technology solutions for farmers, grain buyers, ag retailers, protein producers, and food companies.
I worked for Bushel as a Web Engineer, where I took part in the development of Bushel software and implemented new features such as Cash Bid Services. These services pulled data from the Elevator Transmission Network, stored it in the Bushel software, and provided this data for public access as API endpoints.
In addition to working on Bushel software, I also implemented my own content management system called "Mock Translator" for testing smartphone applications. Company employees could add any data to the Mock Translator and then reuse this data as API responses for their smartphone applications. I used PHP Laravel, java script, jQuery and MySql.

EduTrak

EduTrak Software provides development and delivery of eCommerce, educational, and payment software solutions. EduTrak’s technology solutions improve operational efficiency, enhance administrative productivity, and facilitate information systems at dozens of schools and organizations across North America. EduTrak offers fully customizable payment, registration, and resource management applications.
I have been working for EduTrak since 2018 as a Software Engineer, where I have implemented new features and functionality for the eCommerce software "eStore" and Student Information Systems using open-source and proprietary technologies such as C#, PHP Laravel, jQuery, and Vue.js. I integrated the company's eCommerce software with different payment providers (Authorize.Net, USAePay, Transit, Vericheck) and credit card processing devices (IDTech SREDKey, MagStripe Reader), which helped customers increase sales and provided them with more flexibility to process orders. I have also implemented many other applications, such as a customer support system, funding applications, and point of sale applications.
