Big Iron Registration System

The Big Iron Registration System was developed for North Dakota Trade Office to keep track of how many domestic and international visitors registered for Big Iron Agricultural Exhibition, hosted every fall in West Fargo. NDTO wanted to implement the Big Iron Registration System on their website in order to provide more information about products and services offered by North Dakota companies for Big Iron visitors. At the same time, by means of the registration system, the exhibitors could get more information about the visitors who were interested in their products and services. The registration system consists of public and administration sides. The administration side can be accessed by authorized users, company employees only. From the administration side NDTO employees can view visitors, registered for Big Iron Event; delete any of them; export register visitors to any other format (Excel, Word etc); enable products and services for exhibitors; generate various reports for visitors and exhibitors. The registration system also contains the translation tool that translate the information about foreign visitors into the English language. The public side of the Registration System consists of the registration form where visitors enter their personal information and answer the survey questions about equipment and products they are interested in at the Big Iron Exhibition. After they register in the system they can generate the report with the registration information they provided. The answers that visitors provide during the registration are used by the system to find the best match of companies and products for every single visitor.
The Registration System was developed based on MS Visual Studio 2008, C# programming language and MS SQL Server 2008 database. You can view THE CODE and SQL QUERIES on my github.
